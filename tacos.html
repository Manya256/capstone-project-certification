How to make Tacos

Ingridients

For the Taco Shells (or use readymade ones)
1 cup maize flour (makki atta) or all-purpose flour (maida)
1/4 tsp salt
Water (to knead dough)
Oil for cooking
For the Veg Filling:
1 cup boiled and chopped veggies (e.g., bell pepper, corn, carrot, beans)
1/2 cup boiled kidney beans (rajma) or black beans
1 small onion, chopped
1 tomato, chopped
1 tsp garlic (minced)
1 tsp oil
1 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp red chilli powder or paprika
1/2 tsp oregano or mixed herbs
Salt to taste
Lemon juice – 1 tsp
Toppings (as per taste)
Grated cheese or cheese slices
Shredded lettuce or cabbage
Salsa or tomato ketchup
Sour cream or curd
Jalapeños (optional)
Chopped coriander

Make Taco Shells (Skip if using readymade).


Prepare Veg Filling.


Assemble the Tacos.
