Ingridients

For the Cake (base layer):
1.5 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
1 cup curd (room temperature)
3/4 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup oil or melted butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp baking powder

1–2 tbsp milk (to adjust batter consistency)
(For chocolate cake: add 2 tbsp cocoa powder, reduce flour slightly)
For the Whipped Cream:
1 cup whipping cream (non-dairy or dairy, chilled)
2–3 tbsp powdered sugar (adjust to taste)
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Optional Add-ons:
Chopped fruits (banana, strawberry, kiwi)
Chocolate syrup or ganache
Grated chocolate or choco chips
Glazed cherries or sprinkles
