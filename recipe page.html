How to Make Veg Noodles

Ingredients

1 packet noodles (hakka or instant)
1 tbsp oil (any cooking oil)
1 tsp chopped garlic
1 green chili (optional, finely chopped)
1 small onion (thinly sliced)
1 small capsicum (thinly sliced)
1 carrot (julienned)
½ cup shredded cabbage
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp vinegar
1 tbsp tomato ketchup (optional)
Salt to taste
¼ tsp black pepper powder
Spring onions (optional, for garnish)

Stir Fry Vegetables

1. Heat 2 to 3 tbsp sesame oil in a wok or pan. Add garlic and ginger.
2. Stir-fry for a few seconds.
3. Add chopped spring onions and cook until translucent.
4. Add thinly sliced French beans and stir fry for 2–3 mins.
5. Add shredded carrots, capsicum, and cabbage. Stir fry until almost cooked.
6. Vegetables should retain slight crunch. Do not overcook.
7. Add finely chopped celery (optional).
8. Add 1–2 tbsp soy sauce. Mix well.
9. Add salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything well.
10. Garnish with spring onions if desired. Serve hot!
