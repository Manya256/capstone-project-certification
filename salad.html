How to make salad

Ingridients

1 medium cucumber (chopped)
1 medium tomato (chopped, remove seeds if juicy)
1 small onion (sliced or finely chopped)
1 small carrot (grated or chopped)
1/2 capsicum (chopped)
1/2 cup boiled corn or sprouts (optional)
1–2 tbsp chopped coriander or mint leaves
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Salt to taste
Black pepper powder – 1/4 tsp
Chaat masala – 1/2 tsp (optional)
1 tsp olive oil or mustard oil (optional, for flavor)

Wash and chop all vegetables evenly.


Add them to a large mixing bowl.


Add salt, pepper, chaat masala, and lemon juice.


Mix well. Add olive oil (if using).


Garnish with coriander or mint leaves.


Serve immediately for fresh crunch.
